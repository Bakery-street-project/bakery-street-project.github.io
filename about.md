    <h2>Mission</h2>
    <p>We build practical, local-first AI tooling &mdash; multi-agent systems, LLM automation, and AI infrastructure that runs without cloud dependency. Every project we ship is designed to be self-hosted, auditable, and production-ready from day one.</p>

    <h2>Approach</h2>
    <p>Our stack is polyglot by necessity: <strong>Python</strong> for AI/ML and rapid prototyping, <strong>Go</strong> for performant service backends, <strong>Rust</strong> for systems-level tooling, and <strong>TypeScript/Dart</strong> for frontend and cross-platform clients.</p>
    <p>All LLM integrations default to local inference (Ollama, llama.cpp) with remote fallback. We believe the most valuable AI tools are the ones you control.</p>
